HISTORY OF EARTH

h2>Phanerozoic Eon
570 millions years until Present
  • Palaeozoic Era: Arthropods, fishes, and the first land life appeared.
  • Mesozoic Era: Non-avian dinosaurs ruled, followed by their climactic extinction.
  • Cenozoic Era/Today: Mammals rose, and recognizable humans emerged within the last 2 million years
